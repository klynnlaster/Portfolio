About Me

Hi, I’m Kayanna Laster, a data analyst driven by curiosity and a love for answering questions. My journey into data analytics began during a school project where I discovered the power of Python for analyzing and manipulating data. That project opened the door to endless possibilities, and I’ve been exploring and creating with data ever since.

One of my first personal projects was an exploratory data analysis of a dataset about Elden Ring, a video game I was playing at the time. Using Python, I analyzed data on player encounters with a notoriously difficult boss, uncovering the best weapon types to defeat it. It was a fun and rewarding experience that solidified my passion for data analytics.
